What is MOH DAP?

Ministry Of Health Data Analytics Platform (MOH DAP) , Is an integration and aggregation of health and related data sources that could potentially impact on health. It is a platform for collaboration and sharing of health information with key interest in analysis, prediction, anticipation of diverging and converging health factors. This will in the end support M &E functions for decision making.
